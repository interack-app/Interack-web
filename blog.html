Why are we Indians hesitant about approaching people?

As Indians, we’ve been reserved when it comes to approaching people. Our culture has raised us so that we stay in our shells. And for generations together, we've been following the same. Since social media has seen the light of the day, people are engrossed in their phones so, approaching somebody personally is out of the question. Hitting a ‘follow’ on Instagram might be easy but starting a conversation in DMs is the tricky part.
