Nearby

Socializing just got easier and so did finding their social media handle.

Sometimes, when we come across somebody interesting while going down the street ,cafe or campus, we wish to connect with them but are unable to find a way to do so.

With Interack, you can just find the person right in front of you & get their social media id. Scroll through their social media to know more about them and connect right away! It's time that we make this world more connected and open to possibilities.
